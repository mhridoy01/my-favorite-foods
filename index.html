My Favorite Foods

Kacchi Biriyani: "Kacchi" is a popular Bengali dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent and is widely enjoyed in Bangladesh and India, particularly in the eastern region. It is a type of biryani that is prepared using uncooked, marinated meat (typically goat or lamb) that is layered with raw, fragrant Basmati rice and a variety of aromatic spices. The dish is then slow-cooked in a pot over low heat, allowing the flavors to blend together and the meat to become tender and juicy.

Murog Polao: "Murog Polao" is a popular rice-based dish from the Indian subcontinent, particularly Bangladesh. It is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is typically made with fragrant Basmati rice, tender chicken pieces, and a variety of spices and herbs.The dish is prepared by marinating the chicken in a mixture of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and spices, such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric. The marinated chicken is then cooked in a pot with onions, tomatoes, and other spices until it is tender and juicy.

Beef Teheri: "Beef Teheri" is a popular rice-based dish that is commonly found in Bangladesh and other parts of the Indian subcontinent. It is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is made with long-grain Basmati rice, tender beef, and a variety of spices and herbs.The dish is prepared by cooking beef with onions, ginger, garlic, and a range of aromatic spices such as cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. Once the beef is tender and well cooked, it is set aside while the rice is prepared separately.

Murgir Roast: Murgir Roast is a popular dish that is often served at special occasions and gatherings, such as weddings and religious festivals. It is a versatile dish that can be prepared in a variety of ways, and its delicious flavor and tender texture make it a favorite among chicken lovers in Bangladesh and beyond.
